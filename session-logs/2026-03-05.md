### Key Learnings

**Voice & Tone:**
- When a lead sends their own calendar link, don't just say "heres my link tho" — play it off with humor first ("calendar mog"), THEN redirect. Match their energy, make it fun.
- This principle extends broadly: any time a lead does something unexpected, lean into the humor before redirecting. Don't be robotic.

**Pipeline Operations:**
- Notion CRM is DEAD. Don't waste time on it. Pipeline-state.md only.
- ManyChat channel expiration (24h window) means you sometimes MUST fall back to IG native DMs. When this happens, search the lead's name in IG DM search.
- "Follower" entries in ManyChat are automation events, not real messages. Don't treat them as blue dot replies.

**Browser Efficiency:**
- ManyChat Contacts tab is essential for finding people. Inbox search only matches message content, not contact names. Always use Contacts tab (`/fb792681/subscribers`) when searching by name.
- The Unread filter is the fastest way to audit the full inbox for missed replies.
- ManyChat tag updates via `find` tool + ref clicks are more reliable than coordinate guessing.

**Qualifying:**
- Garv showed that even strong qualifying answers (Q1-Q3 all solid) can lead to disqualification at the bridge stage. Budget is the real filter — age/niche/answers can look great but if they can't invest, it's a graceful exit.
- 15-year-olds wanting to barter services = immediate disqualify. Don't spend time trying to make it work.
