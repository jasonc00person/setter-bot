## Two-Tier Offer (Decided March 19, 2026)

Jason is running two offers under the same funnel. Same DM pipeline, same Calendly, same calls. The tier is determined ON THE CALL based on who the prospect is.

### Tier 1 — Group Coaching (Beginners)
- **Price:** $3-4K for 90 days
- **ICP:** Pre-revenue or early-stage content creators looking to hit first $10K/mo
- **Deliverable:** Group calls, content review, accountability, monetization roadmap
- **Guarantee:** 3 high-ticket clients in 90 days or keep working free
- **Fulfillment:** Group model — scalable, lower time per client
- **Frame:** "Fractional SMM" (not "coaching program")

### Tier 2 — 1-on-1 AI Implementation (Advanced)
- **Price:** $7-10K for 90 days (early bird $3K is DEAD for this tier — underpriced)
- **ICP:** Established operators doing $15K-60K+/mo who need AI automation, systems, fulfillment optimization
- **Deliverable:** 1-on-1 onboarding call, weekly implementation calls, DFY Claude automations, process audit, hands-on builds
- **Guarantee:** Save 20 hrs/week through automation OR double revenue from a specific channel (customize per prospect)
- **Fulfillment:** 1-on-1, DFY heavy — cap at 3-5 clients
- **Frame:** "AI implementation partner" / "fractional AI guy"

### How to Determine Tier on the Call
| Signal | Tier |
|-|-|
| Pre-revenue, wants first clients | Tier 1 |
| Making $15K+/mo, bottleneck is fulfillment/systems | Tier 2 |
| Wants to learn Claude/AI + have things built | Tier 2 |
| Agency with clients, needs automation | Tier 2 |
| "I need content help to grow" | Tier 1 |
| "I need someone to build systems for me" | Tier 2 |

### Revenue Math
- Tier 1: 15-20 clients × $3-4K = $45-80K (group delivery, 6-8 hrs/wk)
- Tier 2: 3-5 clients × $7-10K = $21-50K (1-on-1, more time per client)
- Combined potential: $66-130K per 90-day cohort

**Why:** On Mar 19, Jason had 3 calls (Frederik, Tyler, Maximus) — all doing $17-60K/mo. All wanted AI/automation implementation, not beginner coaching. Jason was trying to fit them into Tier 1 and it felt off. The market is clearly pulling toward Tier 2 for advanced operators.

**How to apply:** On every sales call, read the prospect. Don't default to the beginner pitch for everyone. If they're already making money, pivot to Tier 2 positioning immediately. Never offer Tier 2 work at Tier 1 prices again (Tyler got $3K for what should have been $7-10K).
